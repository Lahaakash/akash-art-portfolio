About

Studio, East London — 2024

"I paint to understand what I cannot say."

Alex Morrison is a London-based painter and mixed-media artist whose practice investigates the archaeology of memory — the way feeling accumulates in objects, textures, and forgotten gestures. Working primarily in oil and encaustic wax, with frequent incursions into printmaking and collage, Morrison's work occupies the space between abstraction and suggestion.

Exhibited internationally across Europe and North America, with works held in private collections in London, New York, and Tokyo. Shortlisted for the Catlin Art Prize and a former resident of the Rele Arts Foundation, Lagos.

Currently based in a studio in Hackney Wick, East London, working toward a new body of work examining industrial entropy and natural reclamation.
